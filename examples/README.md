# Examples

This directory contains example outputs showing what we want to scrape from Google Maps.

## Structure

- **images/** - Sample images scraped from Google Maps (business photos, place images, etc.)
- **text/** - Sample text data (business names, addresses, ratings, reviews, etc.)

## What to Include

### Images
- Business/place photos from Google Maps
- Profile pictures
- Photo examples showing the format and quality we're targeting

### Text
- Business names
- Addresses
- Phone numbers
- Website URLs
- Ratings and review counts
- Sample reviews
- Hours of operation
- Categories/types of business

Add actual examples here as you test your scraper!
